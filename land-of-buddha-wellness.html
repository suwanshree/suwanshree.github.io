Land of Buddha Wellness

Land of Buddha Wellness is a Wix-powered site for a Buddhist wellness center offering courses, events, private sessions, and online payments. The project had to communicate calm and credibility while also supporting bookings, memberships, and operational workflows behind the scenes. The live site is available at landofbuddhawellness.com.

Designing Around Services, Not Just Screens

This kind of site has to do more than look serene. It has to help people discover offerings, understand what is right for them, commit to an event or session, and complete a transaction with confidence. That makes the work a service-design problem as much as a visual one, with every page carrying both brand tone and operational responsibility.

Focus: Service UX, event commerce, and no-code implementation with custom structure.
Stack: Wix, booking flows, payments, subscriptions, and calendar-connected event management.
What it shows: How I translate business needs and user trust into a clear, conversion-ready experience.

Why It Matters

Land of Buddha Wellness demonstrates that design quality is not limited to custom-coded products. I care about matching the solution to the real context, then shaping the result so it feels coherent, usable, and distinctly aligned with the organization behind it.
